# GT2101 Gallery

<p class="lede">
  A collection of high–resolution studio photographs showcasing the <strong>Gale GT2101</strong> turntable — designed by DCA Design Consultants for Gale Electronics.  
  These images capture the perspex and stainless–steel architecture, external control tower, and engineering detail of one of the most distinctive turntables ever produced.
</p>
