<h1>Interview — Ray Churchouse (Sales Manager, 1970s)</h1>

<p>
  A recorded conversation with <strong>Ray Churchouse</strong>, <strong>Ira Gale’s first employee and sales manager</strong>.
  Ray helped establish Gale’s UK retail network and US Air Force PX distribution, and recalls his years working with
  <strong>Ira Gale</strong> during the 1970s.
</p>

<p>
  In this interview Ray talks about the atmosphere at <strong>Hi-Fi-Fo-Fum</strong> in South Kensington, celebrity clients including <strong>Yul Brynner</strong>,
  and how Gale Electronics grew from a small operation into a recognised name in high-fidelity audio.
