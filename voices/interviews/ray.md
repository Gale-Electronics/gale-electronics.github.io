# Interview — Ray Churchouse (Sales Manager, 1970s)

A recorded conversation with **Ray Churchouse**, **Ira Gale’s first employee and sales manager**.  
Ray helped establish Gale’s UK retail network and the US Air Force PX distribution, recalling his years working with **Ira Gale** in the 1970s.
