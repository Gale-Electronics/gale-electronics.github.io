Interview — Ray Churchouse (Sales Manager, 1970s)

A recorded conversation with Ray Churchouse, Ira Gale’s first employee and sales manager. Ray helped establish Gale’s UK retail network and US Air Force PX distribution, and recalls his years working with Ira Gale during the 1970s.

In this interview Ray talks about the atmosphere at Hi-Fi-Fo-Fum in South Kensington, celebrity clients including Yul Brynner, and how Gale Electronics grew from a small operation into a recognised name in high-fidelity audio.
