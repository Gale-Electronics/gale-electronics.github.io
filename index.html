Gale Electronics Archive

Preserving the legacy of Gale Electronics, from the GS401 loudspeakers (engineering by Ira Gale, David Lyth, Billy Woodman; styling by Jon Bannenberg) to the GT2101 turntable (developed with DCA Design Consultants), in a permanent, version-controlled GitHub Pages archive.

This is a historical archive. Includes material preserved from galeaudio.com (2011–2016) with attribution.
